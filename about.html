Spotrot.TTC

Spotrot.TTC is a simple web app designed to help riders of the Toronto Transit Commission (TTC) quickly access information and send messages related to specific stops.

Motivation

This project was born out of a need for a quick and easy way to check the status of a TTC stop (e.g., is it accessible, are there any delays?). I find Google Maps arrivals different from reality sometimes. TXTTTC seems to be reliable for now.

Features

  • Search FunctionalityReplicated google search bar style.Enables users to easily find specific stops by searching for keywords. Enabled Route, Stop name and direction in search
  • Grid of Stops: Displays a list of TTC stops with basic information sorted by distance.
  • Quick Messaging: Allows users to send a pre-filled message containing the stop ID to TXTTTC. *You just have to click on the grid*.
  • Dark/Light Mode: Automatically adjusts the app's appearance based on the time of day for optimal viewing. (**Not Working** its broken and need to look into it once at a later point of time)

Disclaimer

This is not an official TTC app. It is an independent project developed to provide a convenient tool for TTC riders. For official information and updates, please refer to the TTC website.
